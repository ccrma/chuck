    This class implements a helmholtz resonator
    (biquad filter) with a polynomial jet
    excitation (a la Cook).

    Control Change Numbers: 
       - Noise Gain = 4
       - Vibrato Frequency = 11
       - Vibrato Gain = 1
       - Volume = 128

    by Perry R. Cook and Gary P. Scavone, 1995 - 2002.
